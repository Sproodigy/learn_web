<track> kind

Description:
The label attribute specifies the title of the text track.
The title of the text track is used by the browser when listing available text tracks.

Examples

<video width="320" height="240" controls>

<source src="..video/scolopendra_giant.mp4" type="video/mp4">
<track src="subtitles_ru.vtt" kind="subtitles" srclang="ru" label="Russian" default>
<track src="subtitles_en.vtt" kind="subtitles" srclang="en" label="English">

</video>
