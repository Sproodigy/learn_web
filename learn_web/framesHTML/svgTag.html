<svg>

Descriptions:
The <svg> tag defines a container for SVG graphics.
SVG has several methods for drawing paths, boxes, circles, text, and graphic images.

Examples

<svg width="400" height="210">

<rect width="400" height="210" style="fill: blue; stroke-width: 10; stroke: black;" />

<polygon points="100,10 40,198 190,78 10,78 160,198" height="210" style="fill: lime; stroke-width: 5; stroke: purple; fill-rule: evenodd; opacity: 0.7;" />

Sorry, your browser does not support inline SVG.
</svg>
