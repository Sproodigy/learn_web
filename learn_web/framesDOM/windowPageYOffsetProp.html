pageYOffset

Description:
The pageYOffset and pageYOffset properties returns the pixels the current document has been scrolled from the upper left corner of the window, horizontally and vertically.
The pageYOffset and pageYOffset properties are equal to the scrollX and scrollY properties.
This property is read-only.

Examples

Scroll to different levels and click a button.
