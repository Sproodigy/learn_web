groupEnd()

Description:
The console.groupEnd() method indicates the end of a message group.

Examples

Press F12 on your keyboard to view the message in the console view.

<div>

<p> Press F12 on your keyboard to view the error in the console view. </p>

</div>
<script>

console.log("Here I'm!");
console.groupCollapsed("myLabel");
console.log("Hello again, this time inside a group, with a label!");
console.log("Here I'm again!");
console.groupEnd();
console.log("This message is outside the group.");

</script>
